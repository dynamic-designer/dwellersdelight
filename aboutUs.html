About us

Experience the perfect home away from home, designed for your ultimate comfort.

Dwellers Delight Living Ltd Elevate Your Experience with Our Premium Property Services.

Our exceptional team comprises intelligent, skilled, and committed professionals who bring extensive industry experience to building your solution. It is our steadfast dedication to customer service that guides all our actions. Thanks to the relentless efforts of our team and their vision, we have developed a seamless service that places great emphasis on the finer details. Enter a world where you take centre stage, and we support you in delivering that virtuoso performance.

Our leaders make the magic happen! Our managing director, Urvi, is the real maestro of the orchestra. Her incredible skill with finance and technology has led us to incredible heights, all made possible thanks to her corporate genius. When not guiding our team to the next stage in customer service, Urvi is busy indulging in her passion for interior design. Thanks to her many trips all over the world, it is safe to say she knows a thing or two.

No great leader is complete without a trusted right arm, and the other half of our talented management is Kalpesh. As our managing director, he boasts a leadership and client-facing experience. Without him, we would probably be in chaos. One of the first things that you will notice about Kalpesh is his formidable intelligence. With a Master’s degree in Applied Physics and an MBA, he’s led us through some of the formative years of our team. However, it is his love of property which kept him on the Serviced Accommodation path, and we’re glad he’s here!

We’ve had a long and interesting history which we’ve taken to get here. That little story is woven into our souls, and you can read it here on the site. However, it’s our amazing leaders that champion our values of great service and excellence. They’re role models for the whole team, and we love them.
